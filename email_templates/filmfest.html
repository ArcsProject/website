As we are getting closer to our fundraising campaign goal, we have the pleasure to announce that iconic Romanian actress Maia Morgenstern will open the Anniversary Edition of the festival. You may know her for her remarkable performance of Mary, the mother of Jesus in Mel Gibson’s "The Passion of the Christ" (2004). She will bring to Seattle the dramatization of Mircea Eliade’s 1936 novella "Miss Christina".

 

Thanks to your support we were able to invite talented L.A.-based director and actress Elena Beuca with her critically acclaimed debut "D-Love". Inspired by real life events, the film presents an uncanny situation that could nonetheless happen to any of us. Come meet Elena and find out how an unexpected encounter with a total stranger can alter people’s relationships for good.
