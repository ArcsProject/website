2018-2019

After two successful years with over forty students getting Romanian language credits on their high school transcripts, "Româna La Liceu" continues this year in an improved format designed to make it more effective for students that are already proficient in Romanian.

The teaching team, comprised of instructors Ileana Marin, Otilia Baraboi, and Madalina Tonciu wishes you a successful year.

Unique in the US, the program “Româna la Liceu”, launched in 2016 with great success, will include two components: an intensive course of 6 sessions scheduled from October to January and a standard course of 14 sessions from September to April.

The course is targeted at students of Romanian heritage in a context dominated by the English language. The teaching will combine traditional methods (reading comprehension and grammar) with more recent ones (integrative and project-based).

We invite you to confirm your participation in an evaluation of your level of Romanian by September 1st. The evaluation will take place on September 15, from 9:30 AM - 12:30 PM on the University of Washington campus. The exact location will be announced at a later date. The purpose of this evaluation is the placement of the student in the proper course.

If you have not yet paid the registration, please do so as soon as possible. Please send the $20 pre-registration fee before September 15 via PayPal or by check. We also ask that you provide an email address the instructors can use to communicate with you.
