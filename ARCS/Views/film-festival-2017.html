Romanian Film Festival (4th Edition)

One Eye Laughing, One Eye Crying: Tales from Afar

November 17-19, 2017

Opening Night Gala: November 17, 9:00 PM - 10:00 PM at SIFF Cinema Uptown

In a city with a long and dynamic tradition of film-related events, ARCS is thrilled to announce the 4th Romanian Film Festival in the Pacific Northwest: “One Eye Laughing, One Eye Crying: Tales from Afar”, preceded by an extraordinary fusion concert, “Songs from Afar”, cross-listed with Earshot Jazz Festival.

Through a variety of genres (documentary, comedy, drama), we will explore the ways in which Romania’s most recent films relate to the other arts, such as music and literature, thawing conceptual borders and enhancing the old art of storytelling with riveting cinematic makeovers. Local and international special guests will present the movies and interact with the audience at the festival gala.
