The Romanian Film Festival (5th Edition) November 2-4, 2018

Join us in celebrating the 5th Edition of the Romanian Film Festival in Seattle: “One Eye Laughing, One Eye Crying: Uncanny Worlds”, November 2-4 @ SIFF Cinema Uptown.

In tune with the Halloween spirit of the first November week, the theme of this year’s celebratory edition invites you to a visual feast of mesmerizing film features and documentaries exploring the blurred fronteers of reality. This is the right time of the year to take a trip to imaginary, “Uncanny Worlds”, and question our values and beliefs in relation to the unusual, unfamiliar, ineffable, incomprehensible, unfathomable, inscrutable, or the unclear.

You will find out that Romanian films, more than ever, reflect the uncanny aspects of reality, history, politics, everyday life, as well as spirituality, folklore, and traditions; in a word, the uncanny essence of human nature.

Local and international special guests will present the movies and interact with the audience at the festival gala.

To mark this special 5th year anniversary, we invited to Seattle an outstanding guest, our poster artist Daniel Ursache. His creative universe and vision have been exquisitely complementing the movies presented at the Romanian Film Festival over the past four years. We are thrilled to be able to share his work with you in an exhibit entitled “Uncanny Figments”, hosted by A/NT Gallery throughout November. Opening Night and Launch on November 3rd@ 8:00pm.
