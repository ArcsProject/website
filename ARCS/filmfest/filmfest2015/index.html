Past Edition (2014)

A Tale of Success

We brought New Romanian Cinema to Seattle, and the public loved it. The festival's first edition presented feature films, shorts, and documentaries on a sold out venue at the SIFF Film Center. The Q&A sessions with the guest-speakers engaged the public in lively conversations that continued over good wine and Romanian food at the opening and closing-night galas. Great reviews in the media and the energized community carried on the buzz with hopes of a bigger return this year.
